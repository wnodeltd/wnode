# Code of Conduct

wnode is a community‑owned network operated by a professional steward.  
We expect all contributors to engage respectfully, collaborate constructively, and uphold the principles of fairness, transparency, and technical excellence.

---

## Our Standards

### Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and considerate  
- Provide constructive feedback  
- Collaborate in good faith  
- Follow project guidelines and processes  
- Assume good intent from others  

### Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment or personal attacks  
- Discrimination of any kind  
- Disruptive or hostile conduct  
- Sharing private information without consent  
- Bypassing project processes or safeguards  

---

## Steward Responsibilities

The steward:
- Maintains a safe and professional environment  
- Enforces this Code consistently and fairly  
- May take action on unacceptable behavior  
- Acts only within the limits of its operational license  

The steward does **not** own contributor assets, accounts, or governance rights.

---

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact:

conduct@wnode.one

Reports are handled confidentially and respectfully.

---

## Summary

This Code ensures that wnode remains a welcoming, professional, and community‑driven project where contributors can collaborate safely and confidently.
